About The Explo Cafe

Welcome to The Explo Cafe, a fresh start in the same beloved space that once housed 8T8 Cafe. We bring a renewed passion for great coffee and vibrant, healthier meals that don't compromise on quality or taste. Located on Tennant Street, we serve premium coffee and a menu with vegan and dairy-free options.

Premium Coffee
Vegan Options
Healthy Choices

From breakfast to dessert, we focus on nourishing and energizing meals made with the finest ingredients. Whether you're enjoying a quick bite or a relaxing meal, our space is designed to fuel your day and bring the community together.

At The Explo Cafe, we're proud to carry forward the spirit of 8T8, creating moments with healthier options, delicious food, and coffee made with passion in every bite.
